About us

What would you like to know ?

Established in 2022, the YoungGen Entrepreneurs Foundation (NPC: 2022 / 588206 / 08) was founded by Mathuto Adoro, a seasoned entrepreneur, transformation coach, and PhD candidate in Entrepreneurship. YoungGen was created in response to rising unemployment, youth disengagement, and the urgent need to introduce young people to entrepreneurship from an early age. YoungGen also focuses on building resilient and sustainable SMEs, recognising the tough conditions South African entrepreneurs face. By developing their skills, mindsets, and strategic capabilities, YoungGen helps entrepreneurs adapt to challenges and build stronger, more competitive, and future-ready small businesses. The organisation is headquartered in Mangaung, Bloemfontein, and works across schools, communities, and youth development spaces to empower the young generation of entrepreneurs.

We empower and equip learner, youth, and emerging entrepreneurs with entrepreneurship education, leadership development and mindset-shifting support that unlocks real opportunities. We believe that every young person and entrepreneur can build lead and innovate when provided with the right tools, guidance and encouragement.

Since 2022, YoungGen has been transforming the future of young people through practical entrepreneurship education and empowerment programmes. Our journey began with online entrepreneurial classes for learners in Grades 4–12, followed by Saturday physical classes launched at Lebone Village (2022–2023). In 2023, YoungGen hosted its first flagship seminar for learners, youth, and young entrepreneurs — marking a major milestone in our growth. This was followed by a series of youth entrepreneurship seminars, learner empowerment sessions, entrepreneurship tours, and one on one coaching programmes. Over the past four years, our programmes have shaped a movement of confidence, ambition, and action among young people. Learners have rediscovered their dreams, pursued their studies with renewed purpose, and taken bold steps toward their goals. Aspiring entrepreneurs have received mentorship, launched new ventures, and those already in business have shown inspiring growth under our guidance.

    Mathuto Adoro's journey is anchored in purpose, resilience, and a deep conviction that young people deserve opportunities that can transform the direction of their lives. With over 15 years of experience across the public and private sectors, she has applied her expertise in project management, advisory roles, and entrepreneurship coaching to strengthen communities and drive meaningful development. But her calling extended beyond professional excellence. Moved by a passion to see young people rise above their circumstances, Mathuto founded the *YoungGen Entrepreneurs Foundation* — a movement designed to ignite creativity, build confidence, and empower learners, youth, and emerging entrepreneurs to dream boldly and lead with intention. As a mentor, spiritual leader, and speaker, she brings heart, clarity, and vision to every space she enters. Her mission remains simple yet profound: to awaken the entrepreneurial mindset and guide the next generation toward purpose, leadership, and transformational impact.
